2019 AGM Meeting

The meeting took place on Saturday, July 20. It was orderly and well run; thanks to all participants

The new constitution was passed with a very large majority (over 90% in favor)

Tom Morris stepped down from the Executive due to pressing professional commitments and was replace by Adam Gibb who has a long history on the lake

We are making much greater use of the professional services of a biologist to guide us on lake health issues; thus any recommendations we make in the future will have a solid scientific base

TThe AGM focused on lake issues; those issues under the responsibility of the municipality were highlighted and referred to municipal council (the local Councillor was there to acknowledge this aspect and to confirm his availability to deal with those issues)
