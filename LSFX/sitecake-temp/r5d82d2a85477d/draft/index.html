Our Misssion

Our mandate is to preserve the integrity of the lake and keep it healthy and safe.
LSFX represents the people who live on the lake Lac Saint François in Quebec, whether seasonally or year-round.

The mission of the Lake Association is to preserve and enhance the quality of the environment around the lake for present and future generations.

At LSFX, we cherish tranquility, peace, and privacy. We are passionate about our water sports, hiking and biking.
